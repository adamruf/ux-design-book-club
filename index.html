What's this all about?

UX Design Book Club is a fun way for designers to

  • discover awesome new books 📚
  • practice critical & creative thinking 🤔😜
  • quickly grow their experience library 💪🤓
  • have fun networking with other designers 👥😄👥
  • exercise public speaking and presentation skills 👏

How Does It Work?

  1. Vote on the next book to read 🗳️
  2. Winning book is announced 📢
  3. Nine tickets go on sale 🎟️
  4. Milestones are set to
    • Get the book
    • Read your assigned chapter(s)
    • Prepare a 5-minute presentation for the group
    * Milestones, presentation templates, and discussion questions will be emailed to participants. 📖
  5. About 24 hours before the meeting, ticket holders will get a link to join the online book club meeting to meet other designers, share what you learned, and discuss the book you read. 👥😄👥
