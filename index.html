What's this all about?

UX Design Book Club is a fun way for designers to

  • discover awesome new books 📚
  • practice critical & creative thinking 🤔😜
  • quickly grow their experience library 💪🤓
  • have fun networking with other designers 👥😄👥
  • exercise public speaking and presentation skills 👏

How Does It Work?

  1. Vote on the next book to read. 🗳️
  2. Book is announced and sign ups open. 📢
  3. The first nine people to sign up get access to the private online book club meeting. 🎟️ Everyone else is welcome to join the epic public online book club meeting. 🎉
  4. Milestones are set to get the book and finish reading. You can download a worksheet to preview discussion questions and to help you think critically and creatively as you read. 📖
  5. Participate in a 1-2 hour online book club meeting to meet other designers and discuss the book you read. 👥😄👥
